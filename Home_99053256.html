What we do

​YouFarm is a company that specializes in sourcing investments for farmers through their YouFarm Crowd Farming Platform. It addresses the funding needs of farmers and provides them with access to collateral free finance by getting people to invest in crops and livestock and then share the profits with the farmers when the produce goes to the market.

About Us

​The company’s main goals are financial inclusion for farmers in Southern Africa, providing an affordable, safe, insured and profitable investment option for impact investors, ensuring that investors receive a return on investment when they invest in a crop or livestock, connecting farmers to a network of crop investors from around the world, and finally allowing people to become part of the agricultural value chain. Here are some of the duties that the employers at YouFarm:
Registering interested investors and farmers, monitoring crops and farm visits,.
Coordinating and liaising with agronomists across the whole country, project managements and cropping budgets.
